Site Catalog

This type of sites is primarily intended for convenient presentation of a large amount of information, designed in the form of a catalog of goods. The product catalog on such a website is usually more elaborate and detailed compared to a similar catalog that may be located on a corporate website. The Internet directory necessarily has a site search. As for the rest of the content, in general, such a site is not much different from a corporate site, i.e. there may also be news, articles, etc. sections. The design of such a site, as in the case of a corporate site, is focused on business and potential customers - rigor and neutrality, nothing more
